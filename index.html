About Rajaldesar

Rajaldesar is a City and a Municipality in Churu District in the Indian State of Rajasthan. Rajaldesar is a Up-Tahseel of Ratangarh City. The name of this Village is on Bikaner's ruler His Highness Gangbahadur Maharaja Ganga Singh's Mother Rajal Bai.
It is located on national highway NH-11.The distance between State capital Jaipur and Rajaldesar is around 220 kilometers and from New delhi, it is 300 kilometers. The town has a police station which also located on NH-11. Rajaldesar is well connected through Indian Railways and serve a prominent route betwwen Bikaner and New Delhi. There is no airport in Rajaldesar. Nearest International airport is Jaipur airport and IGI airport New Delhi and Dosmestic airport is in Bikaner which does not operate flights regularly.
Rajaldesar has more than five government schools and many private primary and secondary schools for boys as well as girls. The town has also private college for higher study and a cricket or football ground. Rajaldesar has Six Mosque located here and many Temples of Hindu Gods and Dieties.
Rajaldesar is located at 28.03°N 74.47°E. Temperature of the Rajaldesar region in Winter is about -1 °C to 10 °C and in Summer 25 °C to 47 °C. Current Population of the town is unknown as of now.
